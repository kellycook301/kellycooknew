About Me

Kelly Cook here! I am a father, husband, musician, and Maryland native who moved to the Nashville area in 2016 to create a better life for my family. Before making a transition into software development, I worked in the customer service industry for several years. While at Nashville Software School I discovered that I approached creating an app the same way I approached writing a song. While doing both I would always find myself putting great consideration into what the purpose and function of a feature was and how it ultimately served the final product. Those parallels made for some great and very rewarding challenges.
